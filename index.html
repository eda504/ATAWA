ATA-WA: American-Turkish Academy of Washington

ATA-WA is a non-profit educational institution dedicated to teaching children ages 3–14 Turkish language and culture in a fun and engaging environment.

The school runs two semesters: Fall (September) and Spring (February). Classes are held every Saturday morning.
