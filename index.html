ATA-WA American-Turkish Academy of Washington

ATA-WA is a non-profit educational institution meant to teach children between the ages of 3-14 Turkish culture in a fun and lively environment.

School consists of 2 semesters. First semester starts in September, second semester starts in February. Classes are held Saturday mornings.
